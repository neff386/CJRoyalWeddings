About

A history of Royal Weddings

  • 2015

    Welcome Royal Weddings

    Born in Philadelphia to Jesse and Celeste Jenkins, Cathy Johnson was surrounded by flower arrangements and art. Her mother was an avid painter and loves nature. Every season she made sure their small back yard and front porch displayed all of natures beauty. Leaving a lasting impression on Cathy who went on to graduate with a law degree from Georgetown Law and still practices law. Cathy has always taken pride in her gardens. After taking many floral arrangement classes as a hobby she has decided to share her talent with the world.
